How It Works
From setup to billing in minutes
1
Register Your Company
Create a company profile inside GameBill. Give it a name, and toggle it active. You can manage multiple companies from a single account — perfect for business owners who sell to more than one company.
2
Configure Your Games & Rate Cards
Add all the games you offer — any game type, any name. For each game, define a cost rate per point in percentage(%) (e.g., 10% for 100 points). When a customer plays, open GameBill, select the company, pick the game(s), and enter the points or amount value. GameBill instantly computes the exact bill amount/point — no manual calculation required. Review, confirm and save the bill with a single tap.
4
Bill is Saved to the Cloud
Every confirmed bill is automatically saved to Google Firestore — a secure, globally distributed cloud database. You never lose a record. View any past bill from any device, at any time, instantly.
5
Analyze & Export Reports
Head to the Analytics dashboard to see revenue trends, profit/loss, game-by-game performance, and billing history. When you need a formal record, export a detailed Excel report for a specific company or all companies at once — ready to share with your accountant or team.

The Billing Engine
Precise, automatic, multi-game billing

GameBill's billing engine is the core of the platform. It's designed to handle real-world game center complexity: multiple games, different rates, plus you can even add an extra fee to your already existing rates from the company settings — all in a single bill.

MULTIPLE GAME TYPES

Set up as many game types as your center offers. Each game has its own name and its own cost rate per point. Whether it's at $ 1/pt or at $ 0.50/pt, you define the rules.

FLEXIBLE RATE CARDS

Update pricing at any time without disrupting historical records. Past bills retain the original rate they were generated at, giving you accurate historical data while always using the current rate for new sessions.

💡 How a Bill is Calculated

Each bill is generated the moment you enter the points. The system looks up the cost rate for each selected game and multiplies it by the points entered. For multiple games, each game's sub-total is computed separately and summed for the final bill amount.

// Bill Calculation Formula
Bill Amount = Points Used × Cost Rate Per Point

// For multi-game sessions:
Total Bill = Σ ( Pointsᵢ × Rateᵢ ) for each game i

NO MORE MANUAL MATH

Before GameBill, calculating a bill meant pulling out a calculator, multiplying points by rates, adding up multi-game totals, and then writing or typing it somewhere. One mistake and everything is wrong. With GameBill, you enter the points and the bill appears instantly — correct every time, every session.

Cloud Storage & Security
Your data, safe and always accessible

GameBill uses Google Firebase Firestore — one of the world's most trusted, enterprise-grade cloud database platforms — to store all your billing data. Every bill you generate is instantly and automatically saved to the cloud. You don't have to "remember to save" or worry about losing data when you close the app.

Because bills are stored in the cloud and not just on your device, you can view any past bill from any device, at any time — even if your original device is lost, replaced, or switched off.

Firebase Firestore
Backed by Google's infrastructure. Firestore is used by millions of apps worldwide and offers 99.999% uptime guarantees.
Authentication
Firebase Authentication ensures only authorized users can access your company's data. Your bills belong to you, and only you.
Security Rules
Firestore Security Rules enforce that users can only read and write their own data — built-in isolation at the database level.
Encrypted Transit
All data between your device and Firestore is encrypted in transit over HTTPS/TLS — impossible to intercept on the network.
Auto Sync
Data is synced automatically in real time. A bill saved on one device appears instantly on all other connected devices — no manual sync needed.
Persistent History
Every bill is permanently stored. There's no expiry. Access bills from a year ago just as easily as bills from today.

Revenue & Profit / Loss
Know your numbers, grow your business

Revenue and profit/loss tracking is built directly into GameBill — no separate accounting software needed for basic business health monitoring. The moment bills are generated, revenue is tracked. Configure your cost inputs and GameBill will automatically calculate profit and loss for any time period.

// Revenue Calculation
Total Revenue = Σ Bill Amounts for selected period

// Profit / Loss
Profit = Total Revenue − Total Cost

// Profit Margin
Margin % = ( Profit ÷ Revenue ) × 100

WHY P&L MATTERS

Profit and loss data helps owners understand not just how much they're earning, but how much they're keeping. A busy business that spends more than it earns is still losing money. GameBill's P&L visibility helps you course-correct before it's too late.
